TUESDAY OPEN NIGHT

Where:

The Edge. Stanley Place, Cultural Centre, Southbank, Brisbane QLD 4101

When:

Every Tuesday. 5pm - 7pm.

Price:

Free

Hackerspaces are about community, and we'd like to invite you to come visit ours - free. There's no restrictions, no fee and no "right" kind of person; If you're interested we'd love to show you what we've built, what where creating, and here about what you want to make. We generally open from about 5pm onwards and will have dinner at around 7. Sometimes members will have a BBQ onsite for dinner, where anyone can join. So make sure to bring some change!' You don't need to be a member to attend this, feel free to come along and socialize, meet people and talk about awesome ideas.
