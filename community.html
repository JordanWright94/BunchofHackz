What is the Bunch of Hackz Community?

We are a community focused on providing opportunities for those in Brisbane with an interest in programming, design, robotics and much more, to collaborate together, make new friends and develop their skills. What is the Bunch of Hackz culture?

The basic tenets of BoH's culture can be boiled down to two major points:

  • Be excellent to each other.
  • BoH is a Do-ocracy, the best person to effect change is yourself.
