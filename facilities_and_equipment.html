FACILITIES AND EQUIPMENT

About the Bunch of Hackz facilities

We provide a range of facilities and areas to work in, including:

  • Members storage contains 50L lockers for member storage.
  • The Woodworking facility contains all common tools used to manipulate wood, and a lathe.
  • The 30 person teaching room is fully furnished, contains whiteboards, blackboards, and multiple projectors.
  • The general workspace is a large room in BoH, it has many sturdy desks fully equipped with clamps, power points, and anything else you will likely need on a general project.
  • The Warehouse/hangout area is the largest room in BoH. It is a large warehouse with several couches, a sink, and a few cupboards.
  • The co-working desks are located around the facility. They are 1.5M^2 desks with lockable drawers, with adjustable and ergonomic chairs.

About the equipment

We also provide a range of equipment including:

  • electrical power
  • computer servers
  • networking with Internet connectivity
  • machine tools
  • audio equipment
  • video projectors
  • game consoles
  • electronic instrumentation (such as oscilloscopes and signal generators)
  • electronic components and raw materials for hacking
  • and various other tools for electronics fabrication and building things
